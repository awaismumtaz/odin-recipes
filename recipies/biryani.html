Lamb (Gosht) Biryani

Biryani is a bit of a project and is time-consuming, but I have never been disappointed with the results. Since it is such a festive dish, I usually make it for dinner parties. It is a huge hit.

  1. 2½ cups basmati rice
  2. ¼ cup cooking oil
  3. 8 whole cloves
  4. 4 black cardamom pods
  5. 4 cinnamon sticks
  6. 4 large onions, sliced thin
  7. 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  8. 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  9. ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
